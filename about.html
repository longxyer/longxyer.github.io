About me

key words: traveler, photography enthusiasts, optimist, humble

Here is my homepage to my works in architecture and urban design. I finished my bachelor in architecture in China. And in 2015, I came to Europe. I studied in Stockholm for one year and now I'm studying for my second year in Helsinki.

When I think about architecture, images come into my mind. Many of these images are connected with my professional training in the university through the last several years. They come from the books and the knowledge I gathered in the class. Some of the other images have to do with my experience, like the internship.

A stone is thrown into the water, watermark pass out in circle and finally the pond become in peace. As the result, the stone has found its place and the pond is no longer the same. A new building is just like the stone because a building is an intervention in a specific situation. It is essential to the quality of the intervention that the new building should embrace qualities that can enter into a meaningful dialogue with the existing situation. And the existing situation contains not only the physical environment but also the biological environment – human life. So I convinced that a good design must have the ability of absorbing the traces of human life and thus of taking on a specific richness.
