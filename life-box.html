URBAN LIFE BOX

Individual work:

Touchen

02.2014

In the touchen urban project, we have set up 14 projects to help develop the city and my job was responsible for the project which is the urban public space design. Because of the existence of old town, the large urban public space - such as community cultural center, park, etx. - is difficule to intervene. Therefore, in th begining of the project I came up with the idea called micro public space.

In the next step, I mainly paid my attention in looking for the city residents' lifestyle to determine what funcitons I should fill into this micro public space. At the same time I did not ingore tourists' travel habits. I think the urban public spaces are not only designed fot the residents but also for the tourists.

The key word of the micro-public space is: Mobility, The ability of Accumulation and Separation, Variability, Convenience, Leasehold.
