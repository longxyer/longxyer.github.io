JIAAO LIU

SINO-FINNISH COMMERCIAL CENTER

Group work:

ALA partners Juho Grönholm, Antti Nousjoki and Samuli Woolston with Tiina Juuti, Lotta Kindberg, Jiaao Liu, Jasper Lorenz, Rachel Murray, Nea Tuominen and Maciej Warot

Nanjing

09.2016

The Sino-Finnish Center is the competition project I was working for when I was as an intern at ALA Achitects. This project aims to be a representation of an ecologically sustainable way of building cities. The key concept in the design is to think about the ecological impact of the entire life cycle of the development from pre-planning to demolition and recycling.

As for the urban, Sino-finnish center aims to be the commercial center in Nanjing. In this center, there are lots of office rooms, a hotel, several restaurants, a museum and a exhibition center. As a member of this group, I worked for basic information collection and the site model at the first phase. Then I did lots of site analysis and some rough ideas. In the design phase, I was responsible for the traffics and the hotel. And finally I designed and drawed the plans of all the basements and the highrise for hotel.
