Individual work

Hangzhou

05.2013

The project lies in Liangzhu Cultural Town,one of the most innovating and mixed communities inthe city of HangZhou,ZheJiang Province in China,1 hour driving from the city center.This xanadu lifestyle keeps the noisy urban life away from the town on one hand,it nevertheless separate the community from reaching the city on the other.

The project is designed for some cultural workers who want this design to be their workshop and theycan do something special such as cultural promotion because of their social responsibility.The importent things I should think about are: 1.Liangzhu is one of the famous archaeological remains in China which has a 5300 years history;2.there is a school near the site;3.the site is located inthe center of the community;4.the social responsibility of the cultural workers.
