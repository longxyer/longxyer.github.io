JIAAO LIU

HANGZHOU PAINTING MUSEUM

Individual work

Hangzhou

02.2013

This site is in a district called Xixi Heaven which is at the southeast of Xixi Wetland Park.There are lots of luxury hotels in Xixi Heaven which are set for the tourists of the Park.Xixi Wetland Museum is also in this district.And the distance between the Park and this district is about 2.4km.So the transportation between is one of urgent problems.

But there exist some cruise line in the Park.As the result, setting a pier in the site maybe a good solution aboutthe far distance.

Meantime this project ask us to pay our imagination to deal with the relationship between Xixi Wetland and the painting and calligraphy museum. And I found the similarity between the skin texture of Xixi Wetland and calligraphy / painting works.Finally, I choose the seal as the design concept of the museum.
